PROJECT OVERVIEW

AIRBIRDS was created to help users in having a seamless online experience when booking flights. The idea of the app is to generate a secure and easy connections between users and Air travels. Whereby users cannot only book flights but can access other similitudes like pickups and drop offs, reservations and get comprehensive informations like pre travel weather reports, directions from point of booking to destination and other exciting features with just a click.

Imagine hours before your departure time, An official Friendly Chauffeur shows up with a smile offering to help with your luggage and drive you to the airport ?

PROBLEM DIAGNOSIS

We needed to dive deeper into why AIRBIRDS will aid the users to solve a lot of impromptu menace encountered as a result of Air travels and other bookings and how it compares against competitors in flight app's landscape.Most times newbies find it difficult to book flights and oen they can't locate the airport either in form of finding the direction or in form of logistics. In this effect most user tend to miss their flight by arriving late at the airport, and in other cases some arrive too early and aerwards become so weary before their departure time. Lastly we found out that most flight are canceled as a result of bad weather conditions and other technical imbalances.

As about 14 million people travel by air everyday, 25% of this number encounter a challenge and more than half of this challenges are technical issues. In 2014 it was recorded for the highest income loss in Airways industry and this was as a result of technical issues
