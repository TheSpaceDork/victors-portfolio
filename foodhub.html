Foodhub

Simplifying the food order process and accommodating cooking your desired recipe without a kitchen

Most people don't buy the idea of ordering food online because of insecurity and other resourceful reasons.

But how can we be able to solve this problem? Some customers might be allergic to certain ingredients, how can we fix this imbalance?

Project Overview

The idea was to enable a secure connection between users and the restaurant/ kitchen whereby you can order or cook your meal online.

The main goals were to simplify the food order process and create an interface that can accommodate cooking your desired recipe without a kitchen.

Challenges

A lot of people consume improper diet due to the inability to purchase meals with balance diet, Our team is looking to solve this problem with the solutions below;

  • Enable users to select their ingredients while placing orders
  • Provide users with an online kitchen to enable them cook their dishes online at your comfort
  • Provide users with easy and swift delivery process
  • Provide users with variety of vendors and grocery items
  • Design a user-friendly platform

Role

As a team of 4 active members, we were tasked with carrying out the UX research, data visualization, user persona, competitive audit, storyboarding and designing the User interfaces collectively.

Problem Statement

People are engrossed with business and work activities and so they barely have time to spare to cook quality food at home.

Interviews

To have a deeper and more complete understanding of my user ' s needs deep interviews have been made. They have shown me what challenges users face.

User Pain Points

  • Going to the market is very tiring
  • User fears that they may receive unhygienic and substandard items
  • Online products are more expensive and are unnegotiable
  • Late delivery
  • Disclosing card details

User Gain

  • User wants a prompt and affordable delivery
  • User want to shop for dishes without going through much stress
  • User wants a secured platform that doesn't expose their details to scammers
  • User wants to receive hygienic and quality items
  • User wants an easy pricing system
