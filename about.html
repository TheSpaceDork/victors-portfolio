About

Victor currently works at RICHOCOSTUDIOS and resides in Kano, Nigeria.

Before moving to the centre of commerce, he lived in Abuja for two years while working as a Product Designer at Creative Ranch .

Back in Kano where he grew up, Victor guided design on several key features for the research and design of furniture store website,Comforteux

Prior to that, he led design for Airbirds (currently over 1 million users), a flight booking app crafting every necessary informations & activities of travels from the moment of booking to your destination.

Victor likes to go out into the world and capture its beauty and creativity, Sometimes he freelances his skills to create escape room for playing football, workout exercises, travel and other times he takes time to work on his farm land.

Victor also likes collaborating and chatting over a good glass of chapman so get in touch and recommend a mocktail shop
